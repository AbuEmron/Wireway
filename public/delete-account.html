Delete Your Wireway Pro Account & Data

Operated by Jalil Muhammad (operating as Wireway)

You can permanently delete your Wireway Pro account and all associated data at any time. This page explains how to do it and exactly what is removed.

Delete from inside the app

Open Wireway Pro, then go to Settings → Security → Danger Zone → "Delete my account", and confirm by typing DELETE. This permanently deletes your account and all associated data. The action is immediate and cannot be undone.

Request deletion if you can't access the app

If you're unable to sign in, email support@wirewaypro.com from the email address on your account and we'll verify and process the deletion for you.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and profile.
  • All your business data — estimates, quotes, jobs, clients, expenses, mileage, photos, subcontractors, time entries, and billing/draws.
  • Any linked-bank (Plaid) access tokens, which are revoked so no further bank or transaction data can be accessed.

What may be retained

We may retain a limited set of records where required for legal, tax, or financial-record-keeping obligations — for example, payment and transaction records held by our payment processor. Such records are kept only for as long as legally required and are then deleted.

Timing

Account deletion takes effect immediately. Any residual copies in routine system backups expire on the normal backup rotation cycle and are not restored.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: support@wirewaypro.com.
