Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Wireway ("we," "our") provides electrical estimating software at wirewaypro.com. This policy explains what we collect and how it's used.

What we collect

Account information (email, password — stored securely via Supabase authentication), business profile details you enter (company name, contact info, license number), and the content you create (estimates, client records, job photos). Payment processing is handled entirely by Stripe; we never see or store card numbers.

How we use it

Solely to operate the service: storing your estimates, generating proposals, processing payments you initiate, and powering AI features (job descriptions you submit to the AI Quote Builder are processed by Anthropic's API to generate estimates and are not used to train models). We do not sell your data. We do not share your client records with anyone.

Data storage & security

Data is stored with Supabase (PostgreSQL) with row-level security ensuring each account can only access its own records. Connections are encrypted via HTTPS.

Your rights

You can edit or delete your clients and quotes in-app at any time. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us and we'll complete it within 30 days.
